---
title: "Sharing Feedback on Conversations"
---

You can easily review conversations and share detailed feedback directly from the conversation screen. This helps teams improve call quality, speech recognition, response handling, and overall customer experience.

---

## Step 1: Open the Conversation

Navigate to the conversation you want to review. You can fetch the recording and review the transcript before submitting feedback.

---

## Step 2: Give Feedback

1. Click on the three-dot menu (`⋯`) on the conversation card.
2. Select **Give Feedback**.

This opens the feedback popup where you can add comments and rate the conversation quality.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 05 27 At 2 26 08 PM 1](/images/Screenshot-2026-05-27-at-2.26.08-PM-1.png)
</Frame>

### Available Rating Parameters

You can rate the conversation from **1–10** for:

- Speech Recognition Accuracy
- Background Noise Handling
- Response Time / Lag
- Human-like Conversation
- Interruption Handling
- Language Handling

After filling in the details, click **Submit Feedback**.

---

# Viewing Submitted Feedback

All submitted feedback can later be reviewed from the Account Settings section.

## Step 3: Open Feedback Dashboard

1. Go to **Account Settings**
2. Open the **Feedback** section from the left sidebar

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 05 27 At 2 26 58 PM 1](/images/Screenshot-2026-05-27-at-2.26.58-PM-1.png)
</Frame>

---

## What You Can View

The Feedback dashboard allows you to:

- View submitted feedback comments
- Check average ratings
- Review conversation references
- Monitor feedback status
- Track submission dates
- Export feedback using **Export CSV**

This helps teams continuously monitor and improve AI conversation quality.
