## What We Analyze

- Transcripts and summaries
- Intent detection and outcomes
- Sentiment and interruptions
- Objection handling and resolution
- Talk-listen ratio and call durations

## Where to View

1. Go to https://app.uservox.ai and open Analytics or Calls
2. Select a call to view transcript, summary, and extracted fields
3. Use filters (agent, campaign, date range, outcome) to drill down

## Metrics and Scores

- Contact rate, qualification rate, conversion rate
- First-call resolution and follow-up requirements
- Quality indicators: clarity, confidence, objection handling

## Exports

- Download CSV summaries
- Export transcripts for review and coaching
