## Overview

Pulse tracks consumption of AI calling resources by workspace, agent, and campaign.

## Viewing Usage

1. Navigate to Billing or Usage in https://app.uservox.ai
2. Select the date range and scope (workspace/agent/campaign)
3. Review usage by call minutes, attempts, and AI processing

## Alerts and Limits

- Configure alerts at thresholds (e.g., 80% of plan)
- Set caps per agent or campaign to control spend

## Recommendations

- Schedule call windows to optimize connect rates
- Tune qualification to shorten unsuccessful calls
- Use disqualification rules to reduce unnecessary retries
