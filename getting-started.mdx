## **Practice the call before you make it.**

Forge is a voice roleplay trainer for customer-facing teams — **bank employees, CREs, loan officers, insurance advisors.** Pick a scenario, talk to a realistic AI customer over a live voice call, and get scored on how you handled it. No real customer is ever on the line.

## **What Forge is for**

Every scenario in Forge is a **persona on the other end of a phone call** — a skeptical buyer, a first-time borrower, an existing customer you're trying to upsell. Your job is to run the conversation the way you would on the floor: open the call, understand the need, pitch, handle objections, and close.

Forge listens to the whole call and grades it, so you can rehearse a tricky pitch ten times before it ever counts. Typical uses:

- **New-hire onboarding** — get reps call-ready without burning live leads.
- **Product launches** — drill the script for a new card, loan, or rider.
- **Objection handling** — practice the conversations reps lose most often.
