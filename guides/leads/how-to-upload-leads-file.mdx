## Prepare your Excel or CSV file

- **Go to Leads Section**\
  Navigate to **Leads** from the menu.
- **Download Sample Format (Optional)**\
  Download the sample file to understand the required column format (e.g. Name, Phone Number, Email, etc.).
- **Prepare Your Leads File**
  - Use Excel or CSV format (.xlsx / .csv).
  - Ensure all mandatory fields are filled correctly.
  - Phone numbers should be in the correct format.
- **Upload the File**\
  Click on **Upload File**, select your leads file, and upload it.
