### Prerequisites

- You must be **logged in** to your UserVox account.

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### Step 1: Open Account Settings

1. Log in to your UserVox dashboard.
2. Click on your **profile icon / email** at the top-right corner.
3. From the dropdown menu, select **Account Settings**.

![Screenshot2026 01 15at2 10 22PM](/images/Screenshot2026-01-15at2.10.22PM.png)

#### Step 2: Navigate to Members Section

1. Inside Account Settings, go to **Member List / Members**.
2. You will see a list of existing members along with their email IDs and joining dates.

![Screenshot2026 01 15at2 10 32PM 1](/images/Screenshot2026-01-15at2.10.32PM-1.png)

#### Step 3: Invite a New Member

1. Click on the **Invite Members** button on the top-right.
2. A modal titled **Invite a Member** will appear.
3. Enter the **email address** of the user you want to add.
4. Click **Send Invite**.

![Screenshot2026 01 15at2 10 38PM](/images/Screenshot2026-01-15at2.10.38PM.png)

### What Happens Next

- The invited user will receive an **email invitation**.
- Once they accept the invite, they will be added to your UserVox account.
- Their email will appear in the **Members list**.

### Notes & Best Practices

- Ensure the email ID is entered correctly before sending the invite.
- If the user does not receive the invite, ask them to check their **spam/junk folder**.

For any issues, please contact the UserVox support team.
